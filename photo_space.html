THE INSIGNIFICANCE, THE IMPORTANCE
This body of work that puts humans directly in the center of a grand scene, as we are used to seeing ourselves. We don't see how small we are compared to a world full of billions in an infinite universe because we are always the main characters of our own lives.

The world is so big, but we aren't so good at seeing it.
